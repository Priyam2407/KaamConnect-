Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how KaamConnect ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our platform at kaamconnect.in.

1. Who We Are

KaamConnect is an online worker marketplace platform based in India, connecting customers with verified skilled workers such as electricians, plumbers, painters, carpenters, and other home service professionals. Our platform is available at www.kaamconnect.in.

For any privacy-related questions, you can contact us at: priyamyadav2407@gmail.com

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of personal information:

  • Account Information: Your name, email address, phone number, city, and password when you register.
  • Profile Information (Workers): Skills, experience, profile photo, availability, pricing, and identity verification documents.
  • Job & Booking Information: Job descriptions, scheduled dates, locations, and booking history.
  • Payment Information: Transaction records processed through Razorpay. We do not store your card or bank details on our servers.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, search queries, device type, browser type, and IP address.
  • Communications: Messages exchanged between customers and workers on our chat system.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your account on KaamConnect.
  • Connect customers with suitable skilled workers.
  • Process payments and manage the escrow system securely.
  • Send you booking confirmations, job updates, and important notifications.
  • Improve our platform, services, and user experience.
  • Verify the identity and credentials of workers listed on our platform.
  • Prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access to our platform.
  • Comply with legal obligations under Indian law.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal data to any third party. We may share your information with:

  • Workers / Customers: When a booking is made, relevant contact and job details are shared between the customer and the worker.
  • Razorpay: Our payment processing partner receives payment-related information to process transactions securely.
  • Google Analytics: We use Google Analytics to understand website usage patterns. This data is anonymized and aggregated.
  • Legal Authorities: We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to a lawful government request.

5. Google AdSense & Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our website. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites on the internet. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings.

Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to our website. These cookies do not contain personally identifiable information.

6. Cookies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to:

  • Keep you logged in to your account.
  • Remember your preferences and settings.
  • Analyze site traffic and usage through Google Analytics.
  • Serve relevant advertisements through Google AdSense.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some features of our platform.

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These include:

  • Encrypted data transmission using HTTPS/SSL.
  • Secure password hashing and storage.
  • Restricted access to personal data by authorized personnel only.
  • Regular security reviews of our systems.

8. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide our services. If you delete your account, we will remove your personal information within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

9. Your Rights

As a user of KaamConnect, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request correction of any inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time.
  • Lodge a complaint with the relevant data protection authority.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at priyamyadav2407@gmail.com.

10. Children's Privacy

KaamConnect is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we discover that a child under 18 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it immediately.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of our platform after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
