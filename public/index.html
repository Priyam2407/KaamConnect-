The Company

What is KaamConnect?

KaamConnect is a full-stack worker marketplace platform built from scratch in 2026. We connect verified skilled workers — electricians, plumbers, painters, carpenters and more — with customers across Indian cities. Our mission is simple: make hiring local professionals as easy, safe and transparent as booking a cab.

Why KaamConnect

What makes us different

There are other platforms in India, but none built with the same depth, fairness and transparency that KaamConnect offers.

🤝
InDrive-Style Negotiation
Unlike Urban Company where prices are fixed by the platform, KaamConnect lets customers make offers and workers counter — a fair, transparent negotiation just like InDrive.
✅
Admin-Verified Workers
Every worker goes through government ID verification and admin approval before they can accept jobs. No fake profiles, no unverified workers.
🔒
Pay Only After Job Done
Secure escrow via Razorpay — your money stays safe until you confirm the job is complete. Zero risk for customers.
📱
Real-Time Job Matching
Workers get instant notifications for new job offers and can respond in seconds. Our 15-second polling keeps everything live without a heavy WebSocket infrastructure.
💰
Only 10% Platform Fee
We charge just 10% commission — far lower than competitors who charge 20-30%. Workers keep more of what they earn. Customers pay fair prices.
🇮🇳
Built Specifically for India
Designed with Indian workers and customers in mind — supports Hindi/Hinglish in the AI chatbot, focuses on local city-level matching, and understands Indian work culture.
