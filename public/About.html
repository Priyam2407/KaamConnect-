Our Mission

Why we built
KaamConnect

India has over 400 million skilled blue-collar workers. Most of them struggle to find consistent work. Most customers struggle to find reliable help. We exist to fix that — with technology, verification, and fair economics.

⚖️

Fair Wages, Every Time

Our InDrive-style negotiation ensures workers and customers agree on fair prices — no hidden charges, no middlemen eating margins.

🛡️

Safety & Trust First

Every worker undergoes admin verification before joining. ID checks, background screening, and ongoing ratings keep our community safe.

🚀

Technology for Everyone

We build simple, accessible technology so workers without formal education can still thrive in the digital economy.

Our Story

The journey so far

Early 2026
The Idea Sparks
Founder Priyam Yadav notices a neighbour — a skilled electrician — sitting idle for days despite homes nearby needing repairs. The frustration of this disconnect becomes the seed of KaamConnect.
Mid 2026
Building in Public
The first version of KaamConnect is built — a full-stack platform with worker verification, escrow payments via Razorpay, and an InDrive-style negotiation system. Built solo, launched to the first 50 workers.
Late 2026
First 100 Jobs
KaamConnect completes its first 100 verified jobs. Workers earn fair wages, customers rave about the transparency. Word-of-mouth spreads across cities in Punjab and UP.
2027 & Beyond
Scaling Across India
Expanding to 50+ cities, adding regional language support, and launching worker upskilling programs. The mission: every skilled Indian worker should have a digital identity and steady income.
