FAQs

Frequently asked questions

Can't find what you're looking for? Email us at kaamconnect24@gmail.com and we'll help you out.

How do I book a worker on KaamConnect?
Search for the service you need on our homepage, browse available workers in your city, view their profiles and ratings, and click "Book Now". Fill in your job details and confirm the booking through our secure Razorpay payment system.
How does the escrow payment system work?
When you confirm a booking, your payment is held securely in escrow via Razorpay. The worker receives the payment only after you confirm the job is complete to your satisfaction. This protects both customers and workers — zero risk on either side.
How can I register as a worker?
Click "Register" and select "Worker" as your role. Fill in your personal details, skills, and availability. Our admin team will verify your identity and approve your profile, after which you can start receiving job requests from customers.
What cities is KaamConnect available in?
We're currently active in Chandigarh, Prayagraj, Ludhiana, Delhi NCR, Lucknow, and 15+ cities across North India. We're expanding fast — if your city isn't listed yet, register and we'll notify you when we launch there.
How do I report a dispute or problem?
Use the contact form above with "Job Dispute" as the topic, or email kaamconnect24@gmail.com with your booking ID and a description. Our support team responds within 24 hours on weekdays and resolves 97% of disputes successfully.
What is the platform fee?
KaamConnect charges just 10% — the lowest in the industry. Competitors charge 25–40%. We keep it low so workers take home more and customers get fair prices. No hidden charges, ever.
