Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a worker on KaamConnect?
Simply search for the service you need on our homepage, browse available workers in your city, view their profiles and ratings, and click "Book Now". Fill in your job details and confirm the booking through our secure Razorpay payment system.
How does the payment and escrow system work?
When you confirm a booking, your payment is held securely in escrow via Razorpay. The worker receives the payment only after you confirm that the job has been completed to your satisfaction. This protects both customers and workers.
How can I register as a worker on KaamConnect?
Click "Register" on our homepage and select "Worker" as your role. Fill in your personal details, skills, and availability. Our admin team will verify your identity and approve your profile, after which you can start receiving job requests.
Is KaamConnect available across India?
KaamConnect is currently focused on cities in North India including Chandigarh, Ludhiana, Delhi, and surrounding areas. We are actively expanding to more cities across India.
How do I report a problem or dispute?
Email us at priyamyadav2407@gmail.com with your booking details and a description of the issue. Our support team will investigate and respond within 24 hours on weekdays.
